Chapter 15.1: Career Exploration and Goal Setting

This teacher guide provides comprehensive support for implementing Chapter 15.1 on Career Exploration and Goal Setting. The content has been structured to follow the standard format, with Day 1 focusing on foundational concepts and Day 2 providing a hands-on Learning Lab experience.

Teacher guides for both days are available in Markdown format in the teacher directory, and should be consulted for detailed lesson plans.

Key Concepts

  • Distinguishing between jobs and careers
  • Self-assessment of interests, skills, and values
  • Career exploration resources and research methods
  • SMART goal development for career planning
  • Comprehensive career path development
  • Obstacle identification and strategy development
  • Accountability systems for career goals

Assessment Opportunities

  • Self-assessment worksheet completion
  • Quality of SMART career goals
  • Career path timeline development
  • Obstacle analysis and solution strategies
  • Accountability system design
  • Integration of financial considerations
  • Career Path Planner skill builder completion

Differentiation Strategies

For Struggling Students

  • Provide additional structure for activities
  • Offer templates for each component
  • Focus on a more limited range of options
  • Use guided examples throughout
  • Implement more structured peer feedback

For Advanced Students

  • Encourage research into specialized careers
  • Have students create multiple career scenarios
  • Challenge students with sophisticated projections
  • Create digital interactive career plans
  • Develop advanced financial integration models
